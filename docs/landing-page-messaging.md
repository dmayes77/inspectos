- Not another report writer.
- Not another scheduling tool.
- The operating system for running an inspection company.

## Voice and Tone Rules
- Direct, operator-first language.
- Avoid generic “all-in-one CRM” phrasing.
- Emphasize margin, decisions, and trust.
- Keep AI framed as assistive, not replacement.
